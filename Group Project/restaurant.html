RESTAURANT

Enjoy a unique culinary experience at the Hillcrest Hotel and Resort by B.S Group. Add some flavour to your stay as you indulge in an array of authentic and delicious cuisines. Our dining restaurant serves a variety of local and international cuisine. Our expert chefs & cooks ensure the flavours of multi-cuisine food. You can also enjoy mouth-watering grills and barbeques in an elegant outdoor setting. The water served here is pure mineral water and the food & crockery are all steamed before serving and put in clean storage spaces. If you are an absolute food junkie, then La' Chef is the place to be. You can also dine in the privacy of your room and enjoy the attendant comfort of our 24-hour Room Service facilities.
